First Travel

From Sweden to home

My adventure began in the air, heading for Stockholm, but it was on foot and by hitchhiking that I chose to return to Colmar.

For a month, each stage was a story, each route a new chapter.

The spontaneity of hitchhiking offered me unexpected detours, like those five memorable days spent celebrating Midsummer.
Invited by a Swedish family to their farm, I discovered the warmth of local traditions.
From bonfires to folk dances, this unexpected stay immersed me in a rich and welcoming culture.

Beyond the kilometers, it was a real human adventure, punctuated by Nordic landscapes and enriching exchanges.

Second Travel

West America

On our road trip through the national parks of the American West by motorhome, Yosemite was an incredible experience. The towering mountains, majestic waterfalls and giant redwoods were simply awe-inspiring. The hikes and views from Glacier Point were breathtaking.

However, the most memorable moment of our trip occurred in Yosemite. As we sat in our motorhome, just 5 meters from our window, we were lucky enough to see bears in close proximity. It was breathtaking and a truly unforgettable moment in the heart of the wilderness.

Then, while driving along California's Highway 1, we also had the opportunity to film whales with my drone. This experience was truly exceptional and added a touch of magic to our motorhome trip.

Projects

Stellar

Stellar is an atypical holiday accommodation rental site that was my end-of-year project for my Bac +2 exam. It uses Google Maps API to retrieve listings on a map, Stripe for payments, Symfony for the Back-End and twig, CSS and Javascript for the Front-End. There's also an AJAX-based filter search to make it easier to find the ads you want.
